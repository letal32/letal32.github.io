ABOUT

Hi. I'm Marco and I like to describe myself as a passionate learner in the field of telecommunications and web development. Since I was a kid, I had a passion for interconnecting things together. Luckily I was born in the computer era, so it was not particularly difficult to give vent to this passion: IP, routers and switches were my best friends. Far away to consider myself a master in the field, I base my life on learning something new everyday to enrich myself and improve a little bit day by day.

Lately, I've got particularly interested in web development and cloud technlogies. I've been experimenting both with frontend development using HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript and backend development using Python and Javascript. My goal is to explore the integration of IoT networks with the existing Internet infrastracture, thus combining my two passions: Telecommunications and Computers. Feel free to take a look at my projects to have a glimpse at what I'm currently working on.
